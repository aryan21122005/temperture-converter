Formula used

# Celcius Fehrenhiet Kelvin
Celcius C=C °F = (9/5) × °C + 32 K = °C + 273.15
Fehrenhiet C = 5/9(F – 32) F=F K = (F − 32) × 5 ⁄ 9 + 273.15
Kelvin C = K - 273.15 °F = (K − 273.15) × 1.8 + 32 K=K
